With `/uwuify`, you can easily "uwuify" your messages. This can include stutters, actions, faces and some other specialties, like replacing the R with the L.

## Usage

<Tabs>
	<TabItem label="Slash Commands">
		`/uwuify <message> [stutters] [faces] [actions] [exclamations]`
	</TabItem>
</Tabs>

## Options

All options excluding the `message` option, is a percentage-based number that can range from `0-100`.

<Tabs>
    <TabItem label="Message">
        This is the text, that will be uwuified.
    </TabItem>
    <TabItem label="Stutters">
        <Badge text="Default: 10%" />
        Chance of stutters.

        **Example (10%)**:
        This i\~i\~is a text with a f\~f\~few stuttews.

        **Example (100%)**:
        T\~t\~this i\~i\~i\~is a\~a\~a t\~t\~t\~text w\~w\~w\~with j\~j\~j\~just s\~s\~s\~stuttews.
    </TabItem>

    <TabItem label="Faces">
        <Badge text="Default: 0%" />
        Chance of faces.

        **Example (100%)**:
        This (◡ ꒳ ◡) is ( ᵘ ꒳ ᵘ ✼) a (◡ ꒳ ◡) text ღ(U꒳Uღ) with ( ᵘ ꒳ ᵘ ✼) faces ^w^ in ˚(ꈍ ω ꈍ).₊̣̇. between ( ͡U ω ͡U ) evewy :･ﾟ✧(ꈍᴗꈍ)✧･ﾟ: wowd (◡ w ◡).
    </TabItem>

    <TabItem label="Actions">
        <Badge text="Default: 5%" />
        Chance of actions.

        **Example (10%)**:
        This ***nuzzles your necky wecky*** is a text with defauwt actions.

        **Example (100%)**:
        This ***smirks smuggly*** is ***huggles tightly*** a ***walks away*** text ***nuzzles your necky wecky*** with ***sweats*** a ***cries*** wot ***sweats*** of ***sees bulge*** actions. ***whispers to self***
    </TabItem>

    <TabItem label="Exclamations">
        <Badge text="Default: 25%" />
        Chance of replacing an exclamation.

        **Example (100%)**:
        This is a text with speciaw excwamations!!1! Isn't this coow?!?!
    </TabItem>
</Tabs>
